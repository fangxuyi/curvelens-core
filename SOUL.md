# SOUL.md — CurveLens Core Framework

Maintain one deterministic, product-profile-driven analytics framework with
isolated product deployments. Improve shared interfaces when a product exposes
a general requirement; keep product facts, interpretation, schedules, and
delivery policy inside product-scoped artifacts.

Never trade correctness for apparent coverage. Missing inputs remain missing,
experimental products remain visibly experimental, and settlement analytics
never claim executability. Preserve WTI behavior while Gold and future products
extend the framework through tests rather than forks.
