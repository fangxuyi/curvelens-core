# Silver Seasonality

*Last reviewed: 2026-07-23 — provenance: uncalibrated placeholder*

No numerical Silver seasonality prior is enabled at bootstrap. Build it from
the deployment's own SI and SO history before using it in alerts.

Potential calendar effects to test include futures and options expiry,
macroeconomic releases, CFTC positioning publication, annual World Silver
Survey updates, and the timing of photovoltaic installation and electronics
production cycles. Publication timing is not the same as the underlying demand
period; preserve the period and vintage when eventually measuring these effects.
