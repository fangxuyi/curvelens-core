# Russell 2000 Conventions

*Last reviewed: 2026-07-23 — provenance: verified official sources plus authored scope policy*

- The authoritative derivative layer is quarterly CME E-mini Russell 2000
  futures (`RTY`) and matching quarterly options.
- RTY has a $50 multiplier and 0.10-point futures tick.
- IWM and broad sector ETF adjusted closes are context, not synchronized CME
  settlements or exact Russell constituent attribution.
- Basis reflects rates, dividends, time, and frictions; it is not inventory
  carry. Non-quarterly options are excluded at bootstrap.
- Source: [CME E-mini Russell 2000](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/equities/russell/e-mini-russell-2000.html).
