# Russell 2000 Regimes

*Last reviewed: 2026-07-23 — provenance: uncalibrated interpretation priors*

Do not enable fixed regimes without measured RTY history. Analyze price,
breadth, cyclicality, regional-bank sensitivity, credit, realized/implied
volatility, skew, and earnings separately. Credit easing with weak earnings is
a conflict, not automatically a bullish regime.
