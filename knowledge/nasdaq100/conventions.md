# Nasdaq-100 Conventions

*Last reviewed: 2026-07-23 — provenance: verified official sources plus authored scope policy*

- The authoritative derivative layer is quarterly CME E-mini Nasdaq-100
  futures (`NQ`) and matching quarterly options.
- NQ has a $20 multiplier and a 0.25-point futures tick.
- QQQ and sector ETF adjusted closes are contextual, not synchronized CME
  settlements or exact Nasdaq-100 attribution.
- Interpret basis through rates, expected dividends, time, and frictions—not
  inventories. The bootstrap excludes non-quarterly options.
- Sources: [CME Nasdaq products](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/equities/nasdaq.html)
  and [CME NQ facts](https://www.cmegroup.com/trading/equity-index/files/emini-nasdaq-100-futures-options.pdf).
