# S&P 500 Conventions

*Last reviewed: 2026-07-23 — provenance: verified official sources plus authored scope policy*

- The authoritative derivative layer is quarterly CME E-mini S&P 500 futures
  (`ES`) and the matching quarterly options family.
- ES is quoted in index points with a $50 multiplier and 0.25-point futures
  minimum tick.
- SPY and the eleven Select Sector SPDR ETFs are adjusted-close context. They
  are not synchronized official CME settlements or exact index attribution.
- Futures basis reflects rates, expected dividends, time, and market frictions.
  It is not commodity inventory carry.
- The bootstrap excludes daily, weekly, serial, and end-of-month options.
- Sources: [CME E-mini S&P 500](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/equities/sp/e-mini-sandp500.html),
  [CME equity options](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/equities/equity-options-on-futures.html),
  and [S&P sector indices](https://www.spglobal.com/spdji/en/index-family/equity/us-equity/sp-sectors/).

Settlement analysis does not establish executability or confirmed mispricing.
