# S&P 500 Regimes

*Last reviewed: 2026-07-23 — provenance: uncalibrated interpretation priors*

Do not enable fixed numerical regimes at bootstrap. Build them from measured
ES history. Analyze price trend, breadth, realized/implied volatility, skew,
rates, credit, and earnings revisions separately. Broad price gains with narrow
sector leadership and richer downside skew are different from a broad,
low-stress advance even when the headline return matches.
