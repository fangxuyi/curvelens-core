# S&P 500 Earnings and Corporate Fundamentals

*Last reviewed: 2026-07-23 — provenance: verified official sources plus authored collection policy*

Use upcoming earnings as dated event risk and SEC filings as primary corporate
evidence. Distinguish reported results, guidance, capital allocation, M&A,
financing, and management changes. The bounded watchlist emphasizes companies
likely to move the cap-weighted index; it is neither exhaustive nor a licensed
constituent file. Sector ETFs show broad tradable leadership, not exact
contribution to the index return.

Preferred corporate source: [SEC EDGAR APIs](https://www.sec.gov/search-filings/edgar-application-programming-interfaces).
