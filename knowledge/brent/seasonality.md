# Brent Seasonality

*Last reviewed: 2026-07-23 — provenance: uncalibrated placeholder*

No numerical Brent seasonality prior is enabled at bootstrap. Candidate effects
to measure include refinery maintenance, Northern Hemisphere heating and
driving demand, hurricane-related Atlantic disruptions, North Sea maintenance,
OPEC+ meeting timing, year-end inventory behavior, and contract expiry.

Calendar narratives must be tested against measured prices, curves, refining,
flows, inventories, and option surfaces. Publication timing is not the same as
the underlying physical period.
