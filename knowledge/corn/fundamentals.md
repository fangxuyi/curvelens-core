# Corn Fundamental Evidence

*Last reviewed: 2026-07-21 — provenance: verified external source map*

## Collected in the initial deployment

- USDA NASS Quick Stats: national crop condition, development progress, yield,
  production, and planted/harvested area. CurveLens retains report periods and
  treats revisions as new source observations.
- CFTC Disaggregated Futures-and-Options Combined: managed-money and
  producer/merchant positioning for Corn CBOT code `002602`.
- FRED context: broad U.S. dollar, WTI crude oil, and three-month Treasury yield.
  These are contextual channels, not a fitted Corn forecast.

## Required for a fuller production view

- Monthly WASDE U.S. and world balance sheets: beginning stocks, production,
  feed/residual, ethanol use, exports, and ending stocks/stocks-to-use.
- Weekly FAS Export Sales and Export Inspections: new sales, shipments,
  outstanding commitments, destination concentration, and pace versus WASDE.
- EIA weekly ethanol production and stocks plus ethanol/corn crush margins.
- NOAA/NASA gridded precipitation, temperature, soil-moisture, and forecast
  anomalies over production-weighted Corn Belt acreage.
- USDA AMS cash bids/basis, barge and freight indicators, and regional spreads.

Missing feeds must be named in the report. News may explain a move but must not
silently substitute for an absent official observation.
